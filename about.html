About

  • Who built Free Fair Elections ?
    • Free Fair Elections was built by eMoksha using the versatile Ushahidi platform.

  • Why did you build it ?
    • To enable citizens to report on issues during the election process. The content from the reports will be made available for everyone (barring offensive or unsuitable language) without any filtering or modification.

  • Are you affiliated with any Governments ?
    • No. We're non-profits working in the areas of enabling online democracy, clean politics and civic software.

  • We would like to use FreeFairElections for elections in our locality/country. Can you help ?
    • Absolutely. It took approximately 4 person-weeks to set it up for India. Mail us the requirements and we'll get in touch with you within 1-2 days.
